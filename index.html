Who I Am

A story shaped by resilience, community, and purpose.

My journey did not begin in a lecture hall, it began in community: teaching English to fellow refugee students, mentoring young people navigating displacement, and learning that leadership is, above all, an act of service. Today, as a Mastercard Foundation Scholar pursuing a degree in Business Administration with an Accounting concentration at USIU-Africa, I channel that same commitment into finance, entrepreneurship, and social impact.

I believe that sustainable change happens when rigorous professional skill meets genuine community investment. Every fellowship, certification, and project in my journey has been a deliberate step toward becoming a leader who can move fluently between boardrooms and communities, and deliver in both.
