Fellowships & Awards

Recognized for leadership. Selected for potential.

Each fellowship below was competitively earned, a vote of confidence in both my leadership record and my future potential.

Fellowship · 2024–2025

Amahoro Coalition Fellowship — Cohort 4

Selected into a highly competitive fellowship, in partnership with the Mastercard Foundation and the Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation (SDC), representing Rwanda under the theme "Growing Farms, Creating Futures." Led the Mahama Tailoring Empowerment Project as my flagship fellowship initiative.

Millennium Fellowship — "Soles for Success"

Recognized by the United Nations Academic Impact and the Millennium Campus Network (MCN) for successfully completing a Millennium Fellowship Project implemented at Narindi School.
